Why?

The purpose of this test is to create a better font for dsylexic readers.

You, the tester, will play a crucial role in this process. We will use your data from the test to create this font.

Don't stress, take your time, and do your best :)

** Important **

These tests MUST be taken on google chrome due to the font dependencies.

Maze Test Guidelines

The maze test is a series of fill in the blank questions. The tester will be shown a sentence with one word missing from it. In place of the missing word will be a box with three words in it. The objective is to choose the word that you think makes the most sense.

Here is an example question.

Ringo
and
Darryl
took
two
whole
to
change
a
tire.

There is no going back to previous questions. Once you submit, your answer is final.
